October 17th, 1939.

To The Office of the Novayan High Command

The enemy approaches on the Eastern front. Their men will soon be at our gates.

Our army is too weak to go against their forces. They will be marching to their deaths.

In these desperate times, I call upon the Novayan High Command to sanction the use of The Orbital Ion Cannon.

Glory to Novaya Russia.

Signed,
Barkov Pyotr of The Eastern Regiment
